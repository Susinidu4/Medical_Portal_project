Privacy Policy

At MEDIZONE, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you access our online medical portal. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

  1. Information We Collect
    • Personal Information : We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, date of birth, medical history, and any other information you provide to us when registering or using our services.
    • Health Information : We may collect health-related information, including medical records, test results, prescriptions, and treatment plans, to provide you with accurate healthcare services.
    • Usage Information : We may collect non-personally identifiable information about your usage of our portal, such as IP addresses, device information, browser type, and cookies, to improve our services and enhance user experience.

  2. Use of Information
    • Providing Services : We use the collected information to provide medical services, facilitate appointments, deliver test results, and offer personalized healthcare recommendations.
    • Communication : We may use your contact information to send important updates, appointment reminders, and other relevant notifications related to your medical care.
    • Improving Services : We may analyze usage patterns and feedback to improve our services, enhance user experience, and develop new features.

  3. Information Sharing
    • Service Providers : We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as hosting, data analysis, and customer support.
    • Health Professionals : We may share your health information with healthcare providers involved in your treatment to ensure the continuity and quality of care.
    • Legal Requirements : We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our legal rights and the rights of our users.

  4. Data Security
    • We employ appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
    • We use secure data transmission protocols and encrypt sensitive information to ensure its confidentiality during transmission.
    • Despite our best efforts, no data transmission or storage method is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  5. Third-Party Websites
    • Our portal may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites before providing any personal information.
